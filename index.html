Cryptoquote uses a substitution cipher that changes each letter of the alphabet in a famous quotation to a different letter or other symbol. The goal is to solve the cryptoquote before a time limit by buying letters or solving for letters in the quotation.

The player chooses a category and starts the game to display a quotation. Each letter gets a point value. The player starts with 500 points. The player may choose to buy a letter or solve a letter. When they buy a letter, they pay a set amount for the letter choice multiplied by the number of times the letter is used in the quotation.

If the player solves the cryptoquote, the value of all the remaining letters in the quotation is credited to the score. If the player spends all the points before solving the cryptoquote, it is a loss.

A bonus question will be offered after a successful solution that adds 100 points to the score.
